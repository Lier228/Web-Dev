Recipe #1: Pancakes

  • Time: 15–20 minutes
  • Serves: 2–3
  • Difficulty: Easy
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1
Sugar 1 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Mix flour + sugar in a bowl.
  2. Add milk and egg, whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan, lightly oil it.
  4. Pour batter, cook 1–2 minutes each side.

Source: Wikipedia (Pancake)

Recipe #2: Chicken Fried Rice

  • Time: 25–30 minutes
  • Serves: 2
  • Tip: Best with cold rice (from fridge)
Ingredients Quantity
Cooked rice 2 cups
Chicken (cut small) 200 g
Eggs 2
Soy sauce 1–2 tbsp

Steps:

1) Fry chicken in a hot pan until cooked.

2) Push chicken aside, scramble eggs in the same pan.

3) Add rice, mix well, then add soy sauce.

4) Cook 3–5 minutes, stirring so it doesn’t burn.

Source: Wikipedia (Fried rice)

Recipe #3: Greek Salad

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 2–3
  • Best for: Light dinner
Ingredients Quantity
Tomatoes 2
Cucumber 1
Feta cheese 100 g
Olives Handful

Steps:

  • Chop tomatoes and cucumber.
  • Add olives and feta on top.
  • Salt lightly, add olive oil (optional) and mix gently.

Source: Wikipedia (Greek salad)
