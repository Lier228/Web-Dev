Travel to Almaty, Kazakhstan

Want mountains, cozy cafes, fresh air, and unreal views in one trip? Almaty gives you city comfort and wild nature in the same day.

What you’ll see

Snowy peaks, green valleys, and calm lakes near the city.

Top things to do

  1. Ride the cable car to Shymbulak / Medeu area
  2. Walk around Kok-Tobe and see the city from above
  3. Take a day trip to an alpine lake and relax

Places to visit

  • Medeu (mountain skating rink area)
  • Shymbulak (mountain resort)
  • Panfilov Park and Zenkov Cathedral

Food to try

Warm, filling, and tasty: try besbarmak, lagman, and baursaks. And don’t skip hot tea after a mountain walk.
